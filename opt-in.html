Communication Opt-In Consent

By submitting a form on the AZ Water Check website (azwatercheck.com), you are providing your express written consent to receive communications from AZ Water Check, a division of Sol Deja Vu, regarding your water quality inquiry.

What You're Consenting To

When you provide your phone number and/or email address through any form on our website, you consent to receive:

  • Phone calls to schedule and confirm your free water quality test
  • Text messages (SMS) with appointment reminders and follow-up information
  • Email communications regarding your water test results and recommendations

Automated Communications

Some of our communications may be automated, pre-recorded, or use AI-assisted voice technology. These automated systems help us respond to your inquiry promptly and schedule your appointment efficiently.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your inquiry. Typically, you will receive 1-3 text messages related to scheduling your free water test. You will not receive more than 10 messages per month.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply. AZ Water Check does not charge for text messages, but your carrier's standard messaging rates apply.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. You may opt out of phone calls by informing our representative during any call. You may also contact us directly:

  • Phone: (602) 603-4006
  • Email: info@azwatercheck.com

Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely for the purpose of providing water quality services. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Consent is Voluntary

Providing consent is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. You may request a free water test by calling us directly without submitting the online form.
