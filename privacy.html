Privacy Policy

AZ Water Check, a division of Sol Deja Vu ("we," "us," or "our"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website (azwatercheck.com) or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Home address and zip code
  • Water source type and homeownership status
  • Preferred contact time
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

We also automatically collect certain information when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages viewed.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and conduct your free home water quality test
  • Communicate with you about our services, including by phone, text, and email
  • Provide personalized water treatment recommendations
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our business (scheduling, communications)
  • Aquafeel Solutions, our product manufacturer, solely for product warranty and service purposes
  • Law enforcement or government agencies when required by law

Text Messaging and Phone Communications

By submitting a form on our website, you consent to receive text messages and phone calls from AZ Water Check. See our Communication Opt-In page for full details on messaging consent, frequency, and how to opt out.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at info@azwatercheck.com or calling (602) 603-4006.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
